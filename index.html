I am an assistant professor working at SUSTech, Department of Mechanics and Aerospace Engineering.

My research employs mathematical models and large-scale computations to model the behavior of complex multi-physics problems in engineering and biomedicine. Applications include vascular and cardiac fluid-structure interactions, mechanical and biological behavior of soft tissues, and liquid-vapor phase transitions. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computational science and engineering from the Oden Institute at the University of Texas at Austin in 2014. Prior to joining SUSTech, I worked at the CBC Lab at Stanford University as a postdoctoral scholar.

I am hiring research assistant professors and postdocs. I am also looking for doctoral students with strong programming skills and good English ability. Please send me an email with your CV if you are interested. For undergraduate students who are interested in joining our group, please send me a copy of your transcript.
