What am I up to?

Teaching in the Spring quarter just flew by. I've recently added support for distributed dense and sparse-direct Linear, Quadratic, and Second-Order Cone Programs into Elemental and begun integration into more user-friendly tools (e.g., CVXPY). The current Second-Order Cone solver should be reasonably efficient for small second-order cones (e.g., those arising from complex $ \ell_1 $ penalties), but efficient support for sparse SOC problems with large second-order cones is upcoming, and support for distributed sparse Interior Point Methods for Semidefinite Programs is likely several months away.
