Ju Liu is a postdoctoral researcher at the Cardiovascular Biomechanics Computational Lab at Stanford University.

His research interests lie in the broad area of applied and computational mathematics and mechanics. He employ applied mathematics as a rigorous tool to push the envelope of the understanding of multiphysics phenomena across multiple scales and deliver these technologies to both engineering and biomedical applications using high-performance computing.
