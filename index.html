Ju Liu

I am an Assistant Professor at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Department of Mechanics and Aerospace Engineering, located in Shenzhen.

My research focuses on the use of mathematical models and computation to understand complex engineering and biomedical problems. My interests include fluid-structure interactions with biomedical applications, the mechanical behavior of soft matter, and computational inelasticity.
