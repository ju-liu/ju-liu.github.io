I am an assistant professor working at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) at Shenzhen, Department of Mechanics and Aerospace Engineering.

My research employs mathematical models and computation to understand the behavior of complex engineering and biomedical problems. I am particularly interested in fluid-structure interactions with biomedical applications, the mechanical behavior of soft matter, and computational inelasticity
